About Me

I'm Victor Oduopara, a Mechanical Engineering student at Covenant University with a relentless focus on precision design, robotic systems and manufacturing. I don't just model — I engineer with purpose.

From designing a quadruped SpiderBot to engineering a hybrid forklift-trolley and a fully articulated 5-DOF robotic arm, every project reflects my commitment to solving real engineering problems with elegant CAD solutions.

I lead in both technical and community spaces — Co-Lead of the RAIN CU Network and Google Developers Group's Robotics track, actively shaping the engineering culture at my university.
